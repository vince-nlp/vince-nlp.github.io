Multi-document Summarization via Information Extraction

Michael White, Tanya Korelsky, Claire Cardie, Vincent Ng, David Pierce, and Kiri Wagstaff.
Proceedings of the First International Conference on Human Language Technology Research (HLT), pp. 263-269, 2001.

Click here for the PDF version.

Abstract

We present and evaluate the initial version of RIPTIDES, a system that combines information extraction, extraction-based summarization, and natural language generation to support user-directed multidocument summarization.
