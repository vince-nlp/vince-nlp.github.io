CS^2 Makeup Exams


What are the CS^2 Makeup Exams?

If you've already taken one or more of CS 2305, CS 3305, and CS 2340 as non-honors classes, you may want to take the corresponding bridge exam(s). By passing a bridge exam, you will be able to count the three hours for that subject towards the 30-hour requirement to graduate with CS^2. If you plan on taking one or more of the bridge exams in March/April 2023, complete the following form no later than Monday, February 13. It will last 60 to 90 minute and will begin at 5 p.m. on the scheduled date. The exam location is yet to be determined. Note: You are not eligible for taking the bridge exam for a course if you failed it before.

  • CS 2305: Chapter 2 (The Well Ordering Principle) of the the MIT Discrete Math text
  • CS 3305: Chapters 12 (Planar Graphs) and 15 (Generating Functions) of the the MIT Discrete Math text
